Product Design Industry Survey

2016

The series of 3 short surveys represent the largest investigation and seeks to uncover the current state of the product development industry of Ireland. The surveys will look at the challenges of all parties involved; from developers, designers, marketers or any other party involved in creating a product. Whether you're a UX'er, Product Manager, Information Architect or other, we want to hear your thoughts on your industry.

The results will be anonymised, analysed and shared with you in the form of a free in-depth report covering topics such as salary, sentiment, challenges, opportunities, Ireland's position globally and more. Your input is critical to get a complete and fair overview of the industry.

The report will also be provided to the Department of Jobs, Enterprise and Innovation with recommendations on how they can help assist and improve the industry.
